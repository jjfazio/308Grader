  2. Q & A with customer
    • What is the overall navigation and flow like?
      There isn't really a flow, it is more of a spreadsheet and tabs with menu(s) and dialogue boxes.
    • How will the data be stored, located, and accessed?
      It can be accessed from anywhere but changes will only come from local.
    • Do you want an ability to override grades?
      Yes, not all teachers will use this but it should be an available functionality.
    • Do you want email notifications?
      Yes, that could be included. On a related note, there should be some sort of notification system for student users.
    • Are there more users than students and teachers?
      Yes, there are also TA's and graders. Students will be enrolled in the class, or students with general campus wide access.
    • Should there be a tutorial for new users?
      No, there should be some predictive help built in, but that will be discussed later.
