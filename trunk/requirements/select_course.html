2.7.2 Select Course

A student signed in and is presented with a course selector.

Figure 2.7.2 A - Course Selector (most recent Fall 2013)

The course selector has a drop-down list of terms with a corresponding list of classes. By default, the most recent active term is selected. The availability of previous terms is subject to the control of the campus scheduling office.

The student selects the drop-down menu and picks a previous term Fall 2012 and selects CPE 380 from the list of classes.

Figure 2.7.2 B - Selecting a Previous Term and Class from the Course Selector

As soon as a term is selected from the drop-down list on the left, the classes update accordingly on the right. The student selects a course and is directed to his/her grade book for that particular course.

The student selects the drop-down menu and picks the current term Fall 2013 and selects CPE 390 from the list of classes.

Figure 2.7.2 C - Selecting a Current Term and Class from the Course Selector

As soon as a term is selected from the drop-down list on the left, the classes update accordingly on the right. The student is directed to his/her grade book for that particular class.
