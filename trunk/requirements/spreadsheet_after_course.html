2.3.2 Spreadsheet After Course

A student view of the course material spreadsheet in the middle of a course. The spreadsheet, by default, contains all the students enrolled in the course. It also contrains all the different startes of a electronic turin assignment.

In the middle of the course the instructor has made available many assignment with due dates. If a student has not started on an assignment that assignment cell would contain a green hyperlink labeled "start". If the assignment was started, but wasn't completed, the assigments cell would contain a yellow hyperlink labeled "continue". If the assignment is finished that particular cell would contain a blue hyperlink labeled "done".
