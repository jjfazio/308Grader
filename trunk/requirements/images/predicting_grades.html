2.7.4 Predicting Grades

Grade Predictor

At the bottom of the grade book there is a grade predictor. It appears the same as the row in the grade book except some of the fields are editable.

There are four different scenarios the student can experience:

  • Predicting remaining scores based on the kind of final grade the student wants.
  • Fill up a few remaining scores and let the rest of the scores be averaged and predict a final grade.
  • Fill in what the student thinks he/she will get for all assignments.
  • Warn the student if the grade the student wants is impossible to get.

Four Different Scenarios

Here are visuals for the four different scenarios.

  • The second row shows the grades needed for the remaining of the assignments in order to get a particular grade (B)
