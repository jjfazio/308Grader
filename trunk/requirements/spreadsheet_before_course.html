2.3.1 Spreadsheet Before Course

A student view of the course material spreadsheet at the beginning of a course. The spreadsheet, by default, contains all the students enrolled in the course.

At the beginning of the course the instructor has made available the first assignment and is ready to accept submissions through Electronic Handin. When the field names (Studnet ID, Assignmnet X, Midterm, Final) are clicked on they are sorted according to its field data. The studnets row is colored in with a darker gray color.
