2.5.6 View Stats on Spreadsheet

Basic statistics are on the bottom of the spreadsheet. The statistics get updated as grades are entered. Refer to Figure 2.5.1 B for a spreadsheet with statistics. If a teacher wants to view more statistics for a class, they press the View Statistics button at the bottom of the page. This is explained in Section 2.6.2
